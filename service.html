Location Sound Recording

We offer professional audio recording services on location for movies, commercials, TV series and other productions. This includes using high quality microphones, boom poles, wireless systems and other recording equipment to ensure clear and clean audio. A location sound mixer is a key figure in the production of films, commercials, television series and other productions, responsible for capturing all audio on set. Their role is crucial in ensuring that the sound quality is the best for the final product. The Boom Operator is an important member of the sound department in film and television production. They work closely with sound mixers and other producers to ensure clear audio is recorded while keeping the microphone out of the camera's view. They have an ear for detail, technical knowledge of audio equipment, and the physical ability to operate a boom pole.

Field Recording

We provide field recording services for films, commercials, TV series, and more. we capture pristine audio in diverse locations, ensuring your production sounds exceptional. This can be for nature documentaries, wildlife soundscapes, ambient recordings, or any project that requires authentic environmental sounds.

Voiceover Recording

Our voice recording service specializes in providing clear and convincing voice recordings. Provide for commercials, series, animations, podcasts and any other project that requires professional voice recordings. This can include different genres, styles and languages.. With professional studio equipment and experienced voice talent, we ensure your message is delivered with clarity and impact.

Mobile Vocal Booth

A mobile vocal booth is a portable enclosure designed for recording vocals or sound in a variety of locations. It provides a controlled environment with sound isolation and acoustic processing to minimize external noise and reflections, ensuring clean and professional audio recordings. Mobile vocal booths are often used in commercials, film and television productions involving actors to create a controlled acoustic environment on set. This is crucial for capturing clear and accurate dialogue, especially in noisy or acoustically challenged locations.

Location Music Recording

Location music recordings refer to the process of capturing music performances or recordings outside of a traditional studio environment, typically in various locations such as concert venues, outdoor settings, or unconventional spaces. This approach allows musicians and recording engineers to utilize the acoustics and ambiance of different places to enhance or create a specific sound for their music. It involves setting up recording equipment, including microphones, mixers, and sometimes portable isolation booths or screens to manage sound quality and capture the desired atmosphere. The goal is to achieve a unique sound and performance that complements the artistic vision of the music being recorded.
