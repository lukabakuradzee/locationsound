Location Sound Recording

Provides professional audio recording services on location for films, commercials, TV series and other productions. This includes using high quality microphones, boom poles, wireless systems and other recording equipment to ensure clear and clean audio.

Field Recording

Provide field recording services for capturing high-quality audio in outdoor or remote locations. This can be for nature documentaries, wildlife soundscapes, ambient recordings, or any project that requires authentic environmental sounds

Voiceover Recording

Provide voiceover recording services for commercials, series, animations, podcasts and any other project that requires professional voiceover recordings. This can include different genres, styles and languages.

Podcast Recording

Provide dedicated recording sessions for podcasts, providing hosts and guests a controlled and professional environment to record their episodes with high-quality audio.
